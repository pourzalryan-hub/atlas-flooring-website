## SECTION 4 — FIBRE GUIDE

**Background:** `#FAF8F3`  
**H2 (Playfair Display, 38px):** Understanding Carpet Fibres

**Subheadline:** The fibre determines how your carpet performs. We'll help you choose the right one for your lifestyle.

**4-column feature cards:**

**Nylon**  
The gold standard of carpet fibres. Exceptionally durable, resilient, and stain-resistant. Ideal for high-traffic areas and family homes. Holds colour well over time.  
🏆 *Most Popular Choice*

**Polyester (PET)**  
Luxuriously soft and naturally stain-resistant. Great value for bedrooms and low-traffic spaces. Wide range of vibrant colours available.  
💧 *Best Stain Resistance*

**SmartStrand / Triexta**  
Advanced fibre that combines the softness of polyester with the durability of nylon. Built-in stain resistance never washes out. Excellent for households with pets and children.  
🐾 *Best for Pets & Kids*

**Wool**  
The original luxury carpet fibre. Naturally soft, sustainable, and temperature-regulating. Ages beautifully. Higher price point but unmatched in feel and longevity.  
♻️ *Premium & Sustainable*

## SECTION 5 — WHERE TO USE CARPET

**Background:** `#1C1C1C`  
**H2 (Playfair Display, 40px, white):** The Right Carpet for Every Room

**Two-column layout:** Left content, right lifestyle image (couple relaxing on carpet)

**Room guide (gold icons + text):**

**🛏 Bedrooms**  
Plush cut-pile or frieze carpet for maximum comfort. Lighter, softer fibres work well as bedrooms see less traffic. Consider a thicker underpad for that extra-luxurious feel underfoot.

**🛋 Living & Family Rooms**  
Choose a durable fibre like nylon or SmartStrand in a neutral or earthy tone. Loop pile or textured styles hide everyday wear beautifully. Berber works well for modern and transitional looks.

**🏠 Basements**  
Moisture-resistant fibres are essential below grade. We recommend loop pile nylon or synthetic options with a moisture-barrier underpad. Proper subfloor preparation is critical — we handle it all.

**🪜 Stairs**  
See our dedicated Stair Runners page for stair-specific guidance. Patterned broadloom cut to width, or fully fitted carpet, both work beautifully on staircases.

## SECTION 8 — FAQ

**Background:** `#FAF8F3`  
**H2 (Playfair Display, 38px):** Carpet Questions, Answered

**Accordion:**

**Q: How long does carpet installation take?**  
A: A typical bedroom takes a few hours. A full main floor or multiple rooms is usually a 1–2 day project. We'll give you a specific timeline with your quote.

**Q: Do you move furniture?**  
A: Yes — basic furniture moving is included. We ask that you remove small items, breakables, and electronics before our team arrives.

**Q: Can carpet be installed in a basement?**  
A: Yes, with the right product and underpad. We use moisture-resistant fibres and barrier underpads for below-grade installations.

**Q: How do I clean my carpet?**  
A: Vacuum regularly (2× per week in high-traffic areas), clean spills immediately, and have your carpet professionally steam-cleaned every 12–18 months.

**Q: How long does carpet last?**  
A: With proper care, quality carpet lasts 10–15 years. Nylon and SmartStrand hold up best long-term. We'll advise you on realistic lifespan at purchase.

**Q: Do you offer stain protection treatments?**  
A: Yes — we offer professional stain guard treatment at installation, which significantly extends the life and cleanliness of your carpet.
