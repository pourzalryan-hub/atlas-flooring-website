## SECTION 7 — FAQ

**Background:** White `#FFFFFF`  
**H2 (Playfair Display, 38px, charcoal):** Common Questions About Hardwood

**Accordion (each item expands on click):**

**Q: How long does hardwood installation take?**  
A: Most rooms take 1–2 days. A full main floor typically takes 2–3 days including acclimatization. We'll give you a specific timeline during your quote.

**Q: Can hardwood be installed over radiant heat?**  
A: Yes — engineered hardwood is specifically designed for radiant heat. Solid hardwood has limitations; we'll advise you during the consultation.

**Q: How do I maintain my hardwood floors?**  
A: Sweep regularly, use a damp mop (never wet), and use felt pads under furniture. Avoid harsh cleaners. We'll provide a full care guide after installation.

**Q: Can my existing hardwood be refinished instead of replaced?**  
A: Often yes — if the planks are structurally sound and thick enough (3/4" solid hardwood can typically be refinished 3–5 times). We offer refinishing consultations.

**Q: What's the difference between solid and engineered hardwood?**  
A: Solid is one piece of wood — more refinishable, but sensitive to moisture. Engineered has a real wood top layer over plywood — more stable, better for basements and over radiant heat. Both look identical once installed.

**Q: Do you remove and dispose of old flooring?**  
A: Yes — we handle old flooring removal and disposal as part of our installation service. Ask about pricing when you book your quote.
