## PHOTO TIPS

**Shooting installs yourself:**
- Use a smartphone on a tripod — iPhone 14+ or equivalent is sufficient
- Shoot in portrait mode for product cards, landscape for hero and gallery
- Shoot before furniture returns — clean floors with nothing on them
- Shoot from a corner of the room toward the opposite corner (diagonal shows the floor best)
- Time of day: midday or overcast light for even exposure with no harsh shadows

**Getting AI-enhanced versions:**
- For product cards (carpet swatches, wood species close-ups), take a photo of the sample in your showroom
- Use a service like Midjourney, Adobe Firefly, or Luminar Neo to clean up lighting and background
- Describe the product and ask for a "real room scene" upscale

**Using supplier press photography:**
- Shaw: shaw.com/professional/resources
- Mohawk: mohawkflooring.com/trade
- Mirage: miragefloors.com/en/professionals
- Karndean: karndean.com/en-ca/press
- Ask your rep for a press kit — most include high-res room scenes licensed for retailer use
